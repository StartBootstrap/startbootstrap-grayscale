About Us

Grace has brought us safe thus far, and grace will lead us home. We believe that the Bible is the inspired Word of God: holy, inerrant, and infallible. One way of salvation is offered in its pages. Jesus Christ is the way, the truth, and the life. We are saved by grace through faith in Him alone, to the glory of God. Our own works and goodness can not save us. Grace is everything and grace is central.

Freedom is a big part of who we are at New Song Church. As Christians, we have been set free, not for the purpose of license (Galatians 5:13) but for the glory of God in all things (1 Corinthians 10:31). We are freed to worship, freed to grow, freed to serve, and freed to reach.
