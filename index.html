Severus H-Owamparo is a community psychologist, author, wellness ambassador and human rights defender.

His work focuses on the intersecting issues affecting gender variant and sexual diverse individuals regionally and around the globe. These experiences have been pivotal in shaping his vocation and profession.

Severus is the co-director of The Taala Foundation: An entity working to support young people on the margins struggling with mental wellness, through mental health promotion, illness prevention and education in Africa.
