A Series of Paintings

Home is a series of paintings of Kurdistan region of Iraq. The series of paintings are locations that hold memories for both Mohammed Taha and his Daughter Rojan Taha. Each painting is accompanied by Rojan Taha through audio, as seen through her eye's when she was younger and visited these locations.
